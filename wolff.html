Welcome!

We are open daily from 11 am to 5 pm.

Please visit us at Wolff Vineyards in California's beautiful Edna Valley. We welcome wine enthusiasts and families at our small tasting room, complete with picnic facilities.

Enjoy your picnic lunch and our fabulous wines surrounded by the old world tradition of our artisan winery. Spend the afternoon in our vineyard, capturing the panoraic view! We are just minutes from downtown San Luis Obispo and scenic local beaches.

Our Tasting Fee is $10, and refundable with purchase of one of our bottles of wine.

The Tasting Fee for groups of eight or more is $15 per person, and refundable with purchase of one of our bottles of wine.
